Chad Gilchrist - 14865134

Tyler Boyd - 16951977

I am currently in my third year of undergraduate study, working towards completing a Bachelor of Computer and Information Science majoring in Software Development. This paper has introduced me to a lot of new concepts that change the way I perceive my own interaction with computers. In this project, I learned how to properly conduct a usability test, and how to draw useful conclusions from it. I now feel like I have a solid understanding of why good usability is so important in technology. In a job such as Software development, having good typing speed and accuracy can increase the rate you work at drastically. As technology evolves, touch screen tablets such as the surface tablet used in our experiment are becoming increasingly common in both educational and workplace environments. Because of this, it is vital that touch screen tablets and other devices have good usability. In our experiment, a standard physical keyboard performed better on average than the touchscreen keyboard in both typing speed and accuracy. This evidently shows that a physical keyboard is easier to use than a touch screen keyboard. This confirmed my hypothesis from before the experiment was conducted.
Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed my experience working on this project and in this team.
