Why are we conducting this research:

Everyone uses computers these days and every computer needs a type of keyboard that can enter data. People perfer different types; touchscreen, mechanical and membrane. This study will test the different typing speeds and accuracy using a touchscreen keyboard and a basic membrane keyboard.

The touchpad is a fairly new invention and people are still learning the ins and outs of this technology. Touchpads can be very hard to use because of the angle and if it misreads your input. Where membrane keyboards are basic and you can use them for a long time. Some people prefer having a physical object to touch when typing rather than a touchscreen.

Who is this relevant for:

This study will be relevant for students and office workers.

This research will show if its better for students or office workers to use a touchscreen keyboard or a membrane keyboard doing work or assignments.
